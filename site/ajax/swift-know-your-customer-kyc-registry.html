Project Description
JPMorgan, Citi, Bank of America Merrill Lynch, Commerzbank, Societe Generale and Standard Chartered have signed the agreement with Swift to develop a central information register for other banks to help meet increasing compliance demands and costs. Banks within the partnership share information on customers to help meet increasing “know your customer” requirements.
