Project Description
Twitter provides Academic Data Grants to academic institutions; for example, Twitter provided a collection of 2 billion tweets from May 2009 and October 2010 and 1.8 billion tweets collected from August 2011 to November 2012 to Johns Hopkins to track flu infections through coding tweets.
