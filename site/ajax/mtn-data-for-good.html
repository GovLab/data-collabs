Project Description
MTN has established a Data for Good program in which it works with international development nonprofits and organizations on research using mobile phone data. The goal of the program is to help nonprofits leverage access to mobile data toward the Millennium Development Goals. By anonymizing and aggregating their data through Real Impact Analytics, MTN has shared data with CGAP to understand social dynamics involved in mobile money transfers.
