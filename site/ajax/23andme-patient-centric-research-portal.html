Project Description
In general, the partnerships will allow researchers to quickly run queries on more than 1,000 diseases, conditions, and traits so that they can identify new associations between genetic markers using the 23andMe data. Partners like Genentech and Pfizer will access 23andMe research services through a new Research Portal. Pfizer, for example, will use the portal to run custom genome-wide association studies on 23andMe's database of genetic data from more than 650,000 people. (23andMe says more than 80 percent of its customers have consented to add their anonymized genetic data to the database for this type of study.) In the initial phase of the partnership, Pfizer will study 5,000 lupus patients (from the 23andMe community) to better understand the genetics of lupus.
